How to search the database

  1. Click the search bar.
  2. Enter your search.
  3. Press Enter on your keyboard or click the Submit button.
  4. To clear the table click the Clear button.

Example search: “Show me all comedy movies from 2011”

You will then be presented with a table displaying information about the movies.

If there are no movies matching your search some text will display informing you of this.
